About this Initiative


Loneliness and Mental Health Amongst Seniors

Singapore is rapidly aging, and increasingly, more seniors will live alone. We engage seniors through our various initiatives to:

  • Keep them active and engaged
  • Allow for intergenerational learning and bonding
  • Learn new skills
