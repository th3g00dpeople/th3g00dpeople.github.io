Power to the mums

Empowering mothers with diverse abilities and skills

About this Initiative

Being a mum is tough and even tougher for mums with children of diverse abilities, at risk, or journeying alone. This initiative supports these mothers by offering them opportunities to learn a new skill, freelance, and provide them with another income stream.

What We Do

Train the Trainer(s)

We provide specialized training to mums to empower them with new skills. These skills range from soft skills such as facilitation and engagement, to how to operate plastic recycling machines!


Part Time / Sessional Opportunities

The skills they picked up from our sessions open doors to freelancing opportunities and another income stream(s) for their families.


Community Buliding, Mums supporting mums

No one woman is an island. Through our sessions, we journey together, we become a community that helps one another be it through knowledge sharing, support with time, or just being there for each other. Together, we can do more- we do more good, together.
