Her Wellness Project

Empowering feminine hygiene through education and support.

About this Initiative

Her Wellness Project is dedicated to addressing menstrual hygiene and access to sanitary products for low-income communities. By educating participants about proper feminine hygiene and providing essential supplies, we aim to promote dignity, health, and inclusivity.

What We Do

Feminine Hygiene Education

Our workshops cover the basics of menstrual health and hygiene, including practical tips, health awareness, and sustainability in feminine hygiene practices.


Sponsorship of Sanitary Products

Through our partnerships, we sponsor sanitary products for low-income individuals, ensuring no one has to compromise their hygiene due to financial constraints.


Breaking the Stigma

We actively engage communities to foster open conversations about menstruation, breaking the taboos and stigma surrounding this essential topic.
