About this Initiative

Being a mom is tough and even tougher for moms with children of diverse abilities. This initiative supports these mothers by offering them opportunities to learn a new skill, freelance, and provide them with another income stream.

What We Do

Train the Trainers

We provide specialized training to moms to empower them with new skills that they can share with others. These skills open doors to freelancing opportunities and sustainable income streams for their families.
