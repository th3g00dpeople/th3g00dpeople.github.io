About The
Good People


Why did we set up The Good People?

We just wanted to do good things for everyone- the animals, the environment, fellow human beings. We realised that when people do good, they feel good, and they do more good things. So why not make the world a better place by doing good? - Spreading positivity, helping others, and standing up for what's right. #goodvibesonly

Our Mission

To do good, help other individuals do good, and inspire the next generation of changemakers.

Our Story

Established in 2022, we began our journey working with youth at risk and individuals with a history of incarceration. We have come a long way. From introducing befrienders and animal-assisted activities into our sessions to equipping beneficiaries with skills [in other sectors] that empower them to contribute positively to the environment, animals, and their communities, we’ve grown to offer even more opportunities, including part-time internships.

Today, our reach has expanded to a diverse range of demographics, including youth-at-risk, stay-at-home moms, seniors, students, and youth in general for skill-building and character development. We also work with preschoolers and students from lower-income communities, ensuring our initiatives create meaningful opportunities for all. to
