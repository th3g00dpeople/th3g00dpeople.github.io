About The
Good People

Why did we set up The Good People?

We just wanted to do good things for everyone—the animals, the environment, fellow human beings. We realized that when people do good, they feel good, and they do more good things. So why not make the world a better place by doing good? - Spreading positivity, helping others, and standing up for what's right. #goodvibesonly

Our Mission

To do good, help other individuals do good, and inspire the next generation of changemakers.

Our Story

Established in 2022, we began our journey working with youth at risk and individuals with a history of incarceration. From introducing befrienders and animal-assisted activities to equipping beneficiaries with skills, we’ve grown to offer more opportunities, including part-time internships. Today, our reach spans youth-at-risk, stay-at-home moms, seniors, students, and beyond, ensuring our initiatives create meaningful opportunities for all.
